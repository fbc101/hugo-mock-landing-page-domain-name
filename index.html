CrimeGuard

Map application for a safe journey to wherever you need to be!

Get Started

Open-source MIT Licensed.

Real-Time Crime Alerts 🛡️

Get notifications at all times of crime reports both near and far away from you.

Crime Heatmaps 🚀

Easily see where the crime happens most of the time and avoid them! Users can see the color grade to determine how dangerous an area can be.

Anonymous Reporting 📈

Do not be afraid for doing the right thing! Users can report incidents anonymously without facing any threats.

Safe Public Transportation Routes 🦉

Have a quick and convenient access to public transportation schedules and routes while also avoiding dangerous areas.

Historical Crime Data ✨

Users can see what type of crimes have been committed, how recent and how often.

Safe Location Suggestions 🧰

Verified locations that are safe for various activities. Users can rate and recommend places!
